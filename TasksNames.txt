y - Number, that stands for year of university
   (Example: Second year - y2, Fourth year - y4)
s - Number, that stands for semester
   (Example: First semester - s1, Second semester - s2)
p - Number, that stands for project (lab work) number (If it is a modular work, the letter M is added) and letter, that stands for tasks from it
   (Example: First task from Lab №2 - p2a, Third task from Lab №10 - p10c)
